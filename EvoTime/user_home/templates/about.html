Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a premier destination for luxury timepieces

The Beginning

Founded in 2010 by horology enthusiast Michael Everton, EvoTime began as a small boutique in London with a mission to make exceptional timepieces accessible to discerning collectors.

What started as a passion project quickly gained recognition for its curated selection and unparalleled expertise in luxury watches.

Our Growth

By 2015, we expanded to an online platform, bringing our carefully selected collection to watch enthusiasts worldwide. Today, EvoTime is trusted by collectors in over 30 countries.

Our journey is defined by a commitment to authenticity, exceptional service, and building lasting relationships with our clients.
